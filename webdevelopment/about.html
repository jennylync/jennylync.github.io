A Glimpse into my Background

My name is Jennylyn Capones, and I was born and raised in the Philippines. Driven by passion for healthcare and lifelong learning, I moved internationally to expand my knowledge and pursue meaningful work in clinical laboratory diagnostics.
I am certified as an International Medical Laboratory Scientist by the American Society of Clinical Pathologists (ASCPi), and I bring experience from diverse laboratory settings that have shaped my dedication to quality patient care. I am currently pursuing an MHS in Clinical Diagnostic Sciences with an emphasis in CLS along with an Online Educator Graduate Certificate at the University of Missouri. I expect to complete my graduate studies by Spring 2027. My education journey began at Trinity University of Asia in the Philippines, where I earned my Bachelor of Science degree in Medical Technology. Not everyone fully understands the role we play in healthcare so I’m a proud advocate of our profession––because as medical laboratory scientists,We do great things behind the scenes.
